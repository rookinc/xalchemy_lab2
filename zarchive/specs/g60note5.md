Q = MM^T is the effective observable of admissible action.

But Q alone can close too early because it forgets hidden transport data.

The smallest promising repair is a lifted witness

    W_t = (Q_t, chi_t),

with

    chi_t in Z_2^2.

Interpret chi_t as the hidden binary residue class of the transported state.

The zero class 00 is the neutral / fallback class.
The three nonzero classes 01, 10, 11 are distinct primed residues.

Informative action is admissible action that induces nontrivial transport in this hidden residue, not merely visible change in Q.

Thus:
- premature Q-closure is observable fallback,
- nontrivial chi-transport is primed hidden motion,
- genuine lock requires recurrence at the lifted witness level, not merely at the Q level.
