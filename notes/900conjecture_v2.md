# 900 Resonance Conjecture v2

## Status
Working conjecture for the Thalean project.  
Not a theorem.  
Not a stated invariant of the current paper.  
A project-level conjecture about balance and resonance within the native Thalean arithmetic.

## Core correction
The center of gravity is no longer just that 900 appears multiple times.

The stronger and cleaner observation is this:

- the native Thalean scale ladder is 15, 30, 60
- 30 is the exact multiplicative midpoint of that ladder
- 900 is the scalar that witnesses that balance

So the primary claim is now a midpoint-balance claim.
The resonance reading comes second.

The current native Thalean scale family is organized around the quotient tower

G60 -> G30 -> G15

where:
- G60 is the thalion: the concrete 60-state transport organism
- G30 is the intermediate lift layer
- G15 is the quotient-visible algebraic core

This yields the native ladder

15, 30, 60

## Primary claim: midpoint balance
The ladder 15, 30, 60 is multiplicatively balanced:

30^2 = 15 * 60

Equivalently:

900 = 15 * 60 = 30^2

Interpretation:
- 30 is the multiplicative midpoint of the ladder
- 900 is the balance witness of the ladder

This is the strongest current form of the idea.

## Why this matters
The importance of 900 is no longer just that it is a round number or that it can be decomposed in several ways.

Its stronger role is that it is the common scalar obtained by:
- squaring the midpoint layer
- coupling the ladder endpoints

So 900 appears as the scalar that certifies multiplicative balance between:
- core legibility scale: 15
- lift scale: 30
- thalion scale: 60

Interpretation:

900 is not favored because it is visually pleasing.

- 15 from the quotient core

## Working notion of independence
For current project purposes, two realizations of the same number are considered independent if they arise from distinct native strata or distinct native constructions and are not merely trivial algebraic rewrites of one another.

Examples currently treated as meaningfully distinct:
- 15 * 60
- 30^2
- 2 * (15 * 30)

But the first two are now treated as the true core pair.

## Failure conditions
This version would fail or need revision if any of the following occur:

1. The ladder 15, 30, 60 is shown not to be the correct native scale ladder.
2. Another equally native ladder is found that carries the same midpoint-balance structure in a more compelling way.
3. The doubled rectangle route proves to be accidental or non-native.
4. A better candidate than 900 emerges as the true balance witness of the organism.
