## 15. Minimal success condition

The smallest meaningful success for the program would be:

1. construct Qhat_sigma
2. show that at least one coarse family splits under the joint arena
3. identify at least one sign-opposed pair
4. show that one such pair is globally free and one polarity-bearing family is confined

If that happens, the program has crossed from metaphor into mechanism.
