# 900 Resonance Conjecture

## Status
Working conjecture for the Thalean project.  
Not a theorem.  
Not a stated invariant of the current paper.  
A project-level conjecture about resonance within the native Thalean arithmetic.

## Context
The current Thalean scale family is organized around the quotient tower

G60 -> G30 -> G15

where:
- G60 is the thalion: the concrete 60-state transport organism
- G30 is the intermediate lift layer
- G15 is the quotient-visible algebraic core

The primary incidence rectangle on the core is the sector-edge matrix M of size

15 x 30

The present conjecture concerns composite numbers generated from these native scales and primary legibility dimensions.

## Core idea
Some numbers appear only once as ordinary products.
Others arise from multiple independent native constructions.

The conjectural claim is that 900 is the first genuinely overdetermined resonance point in the current Thalean arithmetic.

## Weak form
900 is a privileged resonance number because it has multiple native realizations:

- 900 = 15 * 60
- 900 = 30^2
- 900 = 2 * (15 * 30)

Interpretation:
- 15 * 60 = core × thalion
- 30^2 = lift self-composition
- 2 * (15 * 30) = doubled sector rectangle

## Medium form
Among composites generated from the native Thalean scales and primary incidence dimensions, 900 is the lowest value with at least three independent native realizations.

This is the current preferred form of the conjecture.

## Strong form
900 is not only the first overdetermined resonance number, but marks a genuine cross-phase threshold between the G15, G30, and G60 strata.

This stronger form is speculative and should not be treated as established.

## Why 900 is favored over arbitrary numbers
900 is structurally near the organism's own arithmetic.

It is generated directly from native Thalean quantities:
- 15
- 30
- 60
- 15 x 30

By contrast, a number such as 683 currently has no known native route from the same scale family.

So 900 is not favored because it is round or visually pleasing.
It is favored because it is multiply recoverable from lawful internal structure.

## Comparison with nearby candidates
Current smaller candidates do not appear to match the same degree of overdetermination:

- 225 = 15^2
- 450 = 15 * 30
- 210 = 14 * 15 = 7 * 30

These are interesting, but presently look weaker than 900.

Provisional reading:
- 225 = single-route resonance
- 450 = primary rectangle / incidence area
- 210 = secondary compressed-layer resonance
- 900 = first cross-stratum convergence point

## Working definition of independence
For current project purposes, two realizations of the same number are considered independent if they arise from distinct native objects or distinct strata of the construction, and are not merely trivial algebraic rewrites of the same route.

Examples currently treated as independent:
- 15 * 60
- 30^2
- 2 * (15 * 30)

## Failure conditions
The conjecture would fail or need revision if any of the following occur:

1. A smaller composite is found with the same or greater multiplicity of independent native generation.
2. The currently listed routes to 900 are shown not to be genuinely independent.
3. The admissible generating set is expanded in a way that destroys the current distinction.
4. A better candidate emerges that is more tightly tied to the thalion itself rather than to its quotient-visible shadows.

## Present conclusion
900 should presently be treated as:

- not a theorem
- not a fundamental invariant
- not a primitive constant
- but a strong candidate for the first overdetermined resonance number in the Thalean scale family

## Short form
The 900 Resonance Conjecture:

900 is the smallest nontrivial composite currently known to arise from multiple independent native constructions within the Thalean arithmetic of the G60 thalion and its primary quotient-visible legibility layers.
